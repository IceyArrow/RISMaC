Welcome to RISMaC 2024

Are you ready to test your mathematical skills and compete with the brightest minds in Delhi NCR? RISMaC 2024 is your opportunity to shine! Organized by the Math Club of Ryan International School, Sector 31, this event promises an engaging and challenging experience for all participants.

Why should you register? Participating in RISMaC 2024 offers a chance to enhance your problem-solving abilities, collaborate with fellow students, and win exciting prizes. Join us for a day of intellectual competition and fun!

About

RISMaC is an inter-school team based mathematics contest for high schoolers all based on non-routine fields of mathematics like number theory, geometry, combinatorics, algebra, and analysis. We have multiple exciting rounds in our event which would be a dream for a mathematics enthusiast. We would have teams of 4 participating from various schools throughout NCR, independent teams are welcome with special approval of the school authority.

Power Round

Power round will be a team round and also the longest round of the contest as the questions will be given 24 hours before the contest online. It will be based on an advanced concept which is not a part of high school curriculum, standard or contest. The concept will be explained throughout the document with questions in between. A strict list of standard online tools would be allowed for the competition, which would include WikiPedia, Wolfram Alpha, etc. The exact list would be provided via e-mail before the contest.

Individual Round

Individual rounds will be independent of your team, and will be segregated into 4 subjects, Algebra and Number Theory, Combinatorics, Geometry, and Calculus. A participant is meant to choose 2 of the above and give these in 1 hour windows each during the contest according to the schedule. This would be a speed contest and the only the answers will be written on an answer sheet.

Team Round

Team Round will be a 90 minute standard examination like the individual rounds which would be held during the contest hours. The question paper would consist of comparatively more exciting and less trivial questions which would be meant to be group solved. This would be a proof-based subjective round.

Relay Round

This 60 minute round will be similar to a relay race, problems of various difficulties and points will be assorted on the sheet. Each team would send a runner for a problem who would partially or fully solve a few problems while creating helpful notes for the other runner. Each runner when going back would show the answers to the checker and scoring would happen in real time, he/she would also have to pass their notes regarding a partially solved problem to the coming runner, if any.

Other Activities

Lunch will be provided to all the participants during the refreshments break. Exciting band and dance performances would also be included in the event for refreshing the mind. Attractive prizes would be given to winning teams as well as individuals. Motivating guest speeches by our special guests would be held during breaks. Tie-breaker quizzes may be held if ties happen.
